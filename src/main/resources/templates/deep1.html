12-Week Deep Learning Course Videos

Week 1: Introduction to Deep Learning

Understand the fundamentals of deep learning, neural networks, and AI.

Week 2: Neural Networks Basics

Learn the basics of neural networks, including perceptrons and activation functions.

Week 3: Training Neural Networks

Explore how to train neural networks using backpropagation and gradient descent.

Week 4: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

Understand the principles of CNNs and their applications in computer vision.

Week 5: Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)

Discover the workings of RNNs and their use in sequential data processing.

Week 6: Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) Networks

Learn about LSTM networks and how they handle long-term dependencies in data.

Week 7: Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

Explore GANs and how they generate realistic data in various domains.

Week 8: Transfer Learning

Understand transfer learning and how to leverage pre-trained models for new tasks.

Week 9: Autoencoders

Learn about autoencoders and their use in dimensionality reduction and feature extraction.

Week 10: Attention Mechanisms and Transformers

Discover attention mechanisms and the powerful Transformer architecture used in NLP.

Week 11: Deep Reinforcement Learning

Explore how deep learning integrates with reinforcement learning for intelligent agents.

Week 12: Building a Deep Learning Project

Combine all your knowledge to create a complete deep learning project.
